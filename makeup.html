About Me

Hey, pretty! I’m Qurratu but you can just call me Qurr! 💄✨ Makeup isn’t just a hobby for me it’s my happy place and the little boost I need to feel comfy and confident in my own skin. I’m currently a Mass Communication student (MC112) at UiTM, where I’m learning all about creativity, communication, and self-expression — which goes so well with my love for beauty!

My go-to makeup style? Soft, sweet, and super natural. I’m all about those pinky tones, nude shades, and that effortless “no makeup” makeup look. I love enhancing my features in a way that feels simple, glowy, and 100% me. 🌸
